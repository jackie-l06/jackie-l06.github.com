Project Goals:

My projects will be based on a showcase of my photography. From the feedback session on the Studio 2 project, I have received much helpful feedback that I could continuously work on. I will stay with the visual elements and the ideas I had. I think the idea of camera tapes still works well with the setting of a photography showcase. I think the photography incorporates well with the camera roll as the main setting to browse the photography. As I change the background color to a plain color, I could make the title of the page more stand out. Since I received suggestions about the title and background color from the feedback session. Then I will continue to extend the interactive experience and create something other than scrolling.

Design Strategies:

Since I received feedback about changing the background color of my overall design interface, I think I will plan to have a plain color scheme. The yellow-greenish color makes the overall design less professional. I will try to stay with white or black as my color theme. In addition, I want to update the visual elements for a more illustrated and professional look. The illustration of the camera tape could enhance more details or better color combinations. The visual strategies will focus on a simple and professional overall look. I could change the proximity of each presenting photography and create a better interaction between the user and the photography.

Interaction Strategies:

I plan to updates the interactive experience betwee the user with my photography. The scrolling and clicking could enhance more creative interactions. I will be more considerate on the scrolling part of the photography. The scrollbar was only functioned from scrolling left and right, which might fit Macbook users with the mousepad, but not other types of computer users using the mouse. I plan to incorporate the scrollbar with wedges that make other types of computer users easier to interact with. Other interaction strategies will focus on user feasibility and combine with some interesting visual elements to boost user engagement.
